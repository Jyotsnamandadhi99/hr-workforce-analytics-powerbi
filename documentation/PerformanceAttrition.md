# Performance & Attrition Dashboard

## Objective

Designed and developed the **Performance & Attrition** dashboard to analyze employee performance, turnover, attendance, and termination trends. This dashboard enables HR teams to identify areas of concern and make data-driven workforce decisions.

---

## Dashboard Theme

Maintained the same professional design language used across all dashboard pages.

### Theme
- Dark Indigo background
- Charcoal KPI cards
- Rounded corners
- Consistent typography
- Interactive slicers
- Professional color palette

---

## Dashboard Header

Created:
- HR Performance & Attrition Dashboard

---

## Interactive Filters

Added slicers for:

- Department
- Manager Name
- Performance Score
- Recruitment Source

These slicers dynamically filter all visuals on the page.

---

## KPI Cards

Created four KPI cards displaying key workforce performance metrics:

1. Attrition Rate
2. Average Performance Score
3. Average Absences
4. Average Days Late

Each KPI card follows the project's consistent styling and formatting.

---

## Visuals Created

### Manager Attrition
Visual Type:
- Clustered Bar Chart

Purpose:
- Compare employee terminations across managers.

---

### Performance Distribution
Visual Type:
- Donut Chart

Purpose:
- Analyze employee performance rating distribution.

---

### Attrition by Department
Visual Type:
- Clustered Bar Chart

Purpose:
- Identify departments with the highest employee turnover.

---

### Termination Reasons
Visual Type:
- Horizontal Bar Chart

Purpose:
- Display the most common employee termination reasons.

---

### Performance vs Satisfaction
Visual Type:
- Scatter Chart

Purpose:
- Explore the relationship between employee engagement, satisfaction, salary, and performance ratings.

---

### Absence vs Performance
Visual Type:
- Clustered Column Chart

Purpose:
- Compare average employee absences across different performance ratings.

---

## Business Insights

This dashboard helps answer questions such as:

- Which departments have the highest attrition?
- Which managers experience the highest employee turnover?
- How are employee performance ratings distributed?
- What are the primary reasons employees leave the organization?
- Is there a relationship between employee engagement and satisfaction?
- How do employee absences vary across performance categories?

Successfully developed the third dashboard page, providing HR-focused analytical insights into employee performance and attrition while maintaining a consistent dashboard design across the entire project.
