# Executive Dashboard Layout & Visual Design

## Objective

Designed and developed the Executive Overview page of the HR Workforce Analytics Dashboard by creating KPI cards, visual containers, charts, slicers, and applying a consistent dark theme.

---

## Dashboard Theme

### Background
- Dark Indigo #1A1A2E

### Card Background
- Desaturated indigo #161427

### Text
- Primary: White
- Secondary: Light Gray

### Accent Colors
- #483B6D
- #FFC978
- #FB7185
- #49AA51
- #666666
- #D2E45E

### Filters

### KPI Cards

Designed eight KPI cards with colored accent lines.

KPIs included:

1. Total Employees
2. Active Employees
3. Terminated Employees
4. Attrition Rate
5. Average Salary
6. Average Age
7. Average Engagement
8. Average Satisfaction

Each KPI card includes:

- Rounded corners
- Dark background
- Accent color
- Large metric value
- Descriptive label
