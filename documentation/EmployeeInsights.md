Employee Insights Dashboard

## Objective

Designed and developed the **Employee Insights** page to provide a detailed analysis of workforce demographics, salary distribution, and employee characteristics. This page complements the Executive Overview by focusing on who makes up the organization's workforce.

---

## Dashboard Theme

Maintained the same design language as the Executive Overview dashboard.

---

## Dashboard Components

### Dashboard Header

Created:
- Employee Insights Dashboard title

---

### Interactive Filters

Added dropdown slicers for:

- Department
- Employment Status
- Gender
- Marital Status

These slicers allow users to dynamically filter all visuals on the page.

---

## KPI Cards

Created four KPI cards displaying workforce summary metrics:

1. Average Age
2. Average Salary
3. Average Engagement
4. Average Satisfaction

Each KPI card includes:
- Consistent card styling
- Colored accent line
- Large metric value
- Descriptive label

---

## Visuals Created

### Age Distribution
Visual Type:
- Clustered Column Chart

Purpose:
- Analyze employee distribution across different age groups.

---

### Marital Status
Visual Type:
- Donut Chart

Purpose:
- Display the proportion of employees by marital status.

---

### Race Distribution
Visual Type:
- Horizontal Bar Chart

Purpose:
- Analyze workforce diversity by race.

---

### Department Workforce
Visual Type:
- Stacked Bar Chart

Purpose:
- Compare workforce distribution across departments.

---

### Salary Distribution
Visual Type:
- Histogram

Purpose:
- Analyze the distribution of employee salaries using salary bins.

---

## Data Improvements

Implemented additional enhancements:

- Replaced MaritalStatusID slicer with MaritalDesc for better readability.
- Created Age Group Sort column to ensure logical age group ordering.
- Renamed visual titles for improved clarity.
- Applied consistent formatting across all visuals.

---

## Dashboard Layout

The dashboard follows a structured layout:

Employee Insights Dashboard

↓

Interactive Filters

↓

KPI Cards

↓

↓

## Business Insights

This dashboard helps answer questions such as:

- What is the average age of employees?
- What is the average employee salary?
- How are employees distributed across age groups?
- What is the marital status composition of the workforce?
- How diverse is the workforce?
- Which departments employ the largest number of employees?
- How are salaries distributed across the organization?

## Outcome

Successfully developed the second dashboard page with a consistent visual design and interactive filtering. The Employee Insights dashboard provides HR teams with detailed demographic and workforce analysis to support informed decision-making.
